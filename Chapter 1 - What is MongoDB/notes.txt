MongoDB course chapter 1 Notes

Terms:
1. Replica Set - a few connected machines that store the same data to ensure that if something happens to one of the machines the data will remain intact. Comes from the word replicate - to copy something.
2. Instance - a single machine locally or in the cloud, running a certain software, in our case it is the MongoDB database.
3. Cluster - group of servers that store your data.
Other activities : creating the cluster and connected with the db
